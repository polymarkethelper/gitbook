# Weather Context

The **Weather Context** panel displays weather data for location-specific markets on Polymarket — from extreme weather events to temperature records.

---

## What It Shows

- Current conditions (temperature, wind, humidity, UV index)
- 7-day forecast with daily highs and lows
- **Temperature market ladder** — shows probability breakdown for temperature threshold markets
- Model comparison (where available)
- Links to external weather tools: [Windy](https://www.windy.com/), [Meteoblue](https://www.meteoblue.com/), [Pivotal Weather](https://www.pivotalweather.com/)

---

## Data Source

Weather data is provided by [Open-Meteo](https://open-meteo.com/).

---

## Markets Where This Panel Activates

- [Temperature record and extreme weather markets](https://polymarket.com/weather/temperature)
- [Hurricane and tropical storm markets](https://polymarket.com/weather)
- [Precipitation and drought markets](https://polymarket.com/weather)
- Any location-specific weather event market
