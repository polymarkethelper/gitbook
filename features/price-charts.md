# Price Charts

The **Price Charts** panel brings TradingView-style candlestick charting directly into Polymarket — letting you analyze price history and trend structure without leaving the page.

## Features

### Candlestick Visualization
Full OHLC (Open, High, Low, Close) candlestick charts with real-time updates. The familiar candlestick format lets you quickly identify:
- Trend direction (higher highs / lower lows)
- Support and resistance levels
- Reversal patterns (doji, engulfing, hammer, etc.)
- Volatility clusters

### Multiple Timeframes
Switch between timeframes to see different views of the market:

| Timeframe | Best For |
|---|---|
| 1m | Very short-term traders, in-game markets |
| 5m | Intraday moves and catalysts |
| 1h | Short to medium-term trend analysis |
| 4h | Medium-term structure |
| 1D | Longer-term market view |

### Volume Display
Trading volume is displayed below the price chart as a histogram, showing when large amounts of shares were traded. Volume spikes often coincide with news events or market re-pricing.

### Real-Time Updates
Charts update in real time as new trades occur on Polymarket. You can see the market repricing live as news breaks.

## How to Use It

### Identifying Entry Points
After a news catalyst causes a sharp price move, price often consolidates before continuing. The chart helps you identify:
- Breakout levels to enter a trend position
- Reversion levels if the move looks overdone
- Stabilization zones after high volatility

### Recognizing Distribution / Accumulation
Flat price action combined with high volume often indicates large players accumulating (buying quietly) or distributing (selling into strength). This can precede a larger move.

## Interpreting Common Patterns

**Strong uptrend (green candles, higher highs)**
- Market is pricing in higher probability of YES
- Consider: is there a news catalyst driving this, or is it overdone?

**Steep sell-off (red candles, high volume)**
- New information likely entered the market (bad news for YES holders)
- Consider: is the move based on real information or panic?

**Tight consolidation (small candles, decreasing volume)**
- Market is waiting for a resolution event or catalyst
- Often precedes a breakout in either direction

**Long wicks (candle tails)**
- Rejection at a price level — buyers or sellers pushed back
- Often marks short-term support/resistance

## Markets Where This Panel Activates

Price Charts are available on all Polymarket events with sufficient trading history. The charts display the actual Polymarket odds history — showing how the market probability has moved over time, not the underlying asset price.
