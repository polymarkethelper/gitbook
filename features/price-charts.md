# Price Charts

The **Price Charts** panel brings real-time charts of Polymarket odds history directly into the market page — so you can see how the market probability has moved over time without leaving Polymarket.

---

## What It Shows

- Odds history chart for the current market
- Real-time updates as new trades occur
- Multiple timeframe options to see different views of the market

---

## Markets Where This Panel Activates

Price Charts are available on all Polymarket events. The chart displays the actual Polymarket odds history — showing how the market probability has moved over time, not the underlying asset price.
