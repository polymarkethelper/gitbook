# Live News Tab

The **Live News Tab** shows an X (Twitter) feed relevant to the current Polymarket market — keeping you updated without switching tabs.

---

## How It Works

When you open a Polymarket event page, the Live News Tab loads X posts relevant to that market's topic. Posts come from accounts tracked by PolyHelper and are filtered to match the market category you're viewing.

---

## Markets Where This Panel Activates

The Live News Tab is available on all Polymarket event pages.
