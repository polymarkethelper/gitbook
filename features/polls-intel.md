# Polls Intel

The **Polls Intel** panel shows polling data for election and political markets on Polymarket — letting you compare where polls stand against current market odds.

---

## What It Shows

Polling averages for the relevant election or political question:
- Current polling numbers for each candidate or option
- Trend over time
- Comparison to current Polymarket odds

---

## Markets Where This Panel Activates

- [US Presidential, Senate, and House elections](https://polymarket.com/politics)
- [International elections](https://polymarket.com/politics)
- Approval rating markets
- [2026 Midterms](https://polymarket.com/event/balance-of-power-2026-midterms)
- Any political market where polling data is relevant
