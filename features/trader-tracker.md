# Trader Tracker

The **Trader Tracker** lets you follow specific Polymarket traders, organize them into color-coded groups, and monitor their live trading activity.

---

## How to Add a Trader

Every comment in Polymarket markets gets a small track button next to the timestamp. Click it to add that trader to your Tracker and assign them to a group.

---

## Groups

Organize tracked traders into custom named groups with color labels. You can:
- Create up to multiple groups
- Rename or recolor groups
- Delete groups (as long as at least one group remains)

Each group shows member avatars and the trader count.

---

## Trader Size Tiers

Traders are automatically classified by position size:

| Tier | Size |
|---|---|
| 🐋 WHALE | ≥ $50,000 |
| MID | $10,000 – $50,000 |
| SMALL | $1,000 – $10,000 |

---

## Live Activity Feed

When a tracked trader makes a move, you see:
- BUY or SELL
- Dollar amount
- Market and outcome
- Timestamp

A visual bar shows the buy/sell split for each trader.

---

## Works With Comments Filter

Tracked traders appear in the [Comments Filter](comments-filter.md) dropdown — letting you filter market comments to show only their posts.

---

## Markets Where This Feature Activates

Trader Tracker works across all Polymarket markets — it's a global tool, not market-specific.
