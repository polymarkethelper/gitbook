# Stock Context

The **Stock Context** panel provides market data for Polymarket events tied to publicly traded companies — earnings outcomes, stock price targets, and company-related markets.

---

## What It Shows

Company analytics, earnings data, and holdings for stock-related markets. The panel automatically detects which ticker is relevant to the current market and loads the corresponding data.

**Supported tickers include:** AAPL, MSFT, TSLA, NVDA, GOOGL, AMZN, META, NFLX, AMD, INTC, DIS, BA, JPM, GS, V, WMT, KO, PFE, MRNA, PLTR, COIN, GME, SPY, QQQ, MSTR, and more.

### Analyst Consensus

Wall Street analyst price target consensus — shows the min/max target range and number of analysts covering the stock. Useful for calibrating whether Polymarket's implied probability on a price target market is in line with professional estimates.

---

## Markets Where This Panel Activates

- [Earnings beat/miss markets](https://polymarket.com/finance)
- [Stock price target markets](https://polymarket.com/finance)
- M&A and acquisition markets
- Any market involving a publicly traded company
