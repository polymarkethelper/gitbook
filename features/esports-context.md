# Esports Context

The **Esports Context** panel brings competitive gaming data into Polymarket for esports markets.

---

## What It Shows

- Team standings in the relevant league or tournament
- Recent match results
- Head-to-head history between teams
- Tournament bracket and stage context

---

## Markets Where This Panel Activates

- LoL, Valorant, Dota 2, CS2, CoD, MLBB, Overwatch, and Rainbow Six Siege match markets
- Tournament winner and placement markets
