# Esports Context

The **Esports Context** panel brings competitive gaming data into Polymarket for esports markets.

![Esports Context Panel](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/polymarkethelper/gitbook/main/.gitbook/assets/esports-context.png)

---

## What It Shows

- Team standings in the relevant league or tournament
- Recent match results
- Head-to-head history between teams
- Tournament bracket and stage context

---

## Markets Where This Panel Activates

- [LoL](https://polymarket.com/esports/league-of-legends), [Valorant](https://polymarket.com/esports/valorant/games), [CS2](https://polymarket.com/esports/cs2), CoD, MLBB, Overwatch, and Rainbow Six Siege match markets
- [Tournament winner and placement markets](https://polymarket.com/esports)
